BIO_DATA

I'm a mechatronics engineer based in Amman, Jordan who loves the intersection of hardware and software. My work lives at the boundary where circuits meet code — writing firmware for microcontrollers, designing PCBs, and building robots.

I got into embedded systems because I was fascinated by how a few lines of C could make a motor spin at exactly the right speed, or make a sensor network report back live data from across a field. That magic still drives me.

When I'm not at my desk, you'll find me soldering something, reading datasheets (yes, for fun), or arguing about the correct way to do I2C pull-up calculations.
